Max Hammerlund

I'm a senior at the University of Minnesota, Twin Cities. I'm the webmaster for the University of Minnesota's Video Game Development Club (VGDC).

I have been the lead programmer of VGDC for the past three semesters, during which we've made three games. I've been a member of VGDC since my first semester of college. I've participated in the Global Game Jam for the past three years, along with several game jams run by VGDC.

I am persuing a career in game development. My main interest areas are artificial intelligence, user interface, and engine programming, but I'm interested in any game programming position.
